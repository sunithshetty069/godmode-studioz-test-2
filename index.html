Our Story

Built to define
first impressions.

India's first dedicated multimedia production studio — where every cut, card, poster, and animation is crafted to shape a story's opening moment.

GodMode Studioz was born from a singular belief: the first experience of a film determines everything that follows. How an audience feels in that first teaser, that first poster — that emotion drives curiosity, builds anticipation, and shapes the audience before the film even releases.

We offer the full spectrum of film promotional visuals — from early conceptual promos to teasers, trailers, motion graphics, title design, and complete campaign ecosystems. All from one studio, in Bengaluru.

With over 5 years of experience, we have worked across Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, Spanish, and English — delivering 80+ projects spanning films, OTT originals, and major promotional campaigns for India's most celebrated productions.

Through powerful edits, cinematic storytelling, and high-impact motion, we ensure every project receives a strong, emotional, and memorable visual identity.
